Upgrades

Upgrades, from the word itself, it is a power-up in stats that gives significant boost to players that boost net worth production and convenience.

Shop Space

Shop space boost the amount of items you can sell to NPC's and players at a time

Carry Space

Carry space boost the amount of items you can carry from the auctions you won

Inventory Space

Inventory space boost the amount of items you can store at a time

Paycheck Amount

Paycheck amount boost the amount of gems you get by staying every 20 minutes ingame

Remember that all of these upgrades significantly helps to player's rate of net worth, inventory space being the most expensive of them all and paycheck amount being the cheapest.
