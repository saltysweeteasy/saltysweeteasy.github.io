Auctions

Bid Battles revolves around Auctions. Auctions are where you buy garages with items within them, you are given a specific amount of budget to how much worth of garages you can buy, the items you get can sold after the Auction is over and you can choose to keep 2-? amount of items (Depending on your carry space) every Auction to sell on your store. Auctions in Bid Battles have 3 categories, Cash Auctions, Gem Auctions, and Limited Auctions. Each having a difference whether in Entry Fee, item rarities, etc.

Currently, there are 13 cash auctions to choose from. Also, for most auctions, the amount required to participate is also the amount available as budget in the auction. As an example in the Beverly Hills auction you need 25,000 cash and in the auction you have a budget of 25,000, however this does not apply to the Sky auction because the amount required to participate is 15,000,000 cash, but in the auction you have a budget of 6,000,000 cash.
