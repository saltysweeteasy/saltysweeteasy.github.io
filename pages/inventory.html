Inventory

Self explanatory, Inventory is where you store your items you got from auctions, and trading! It is crucial for storing items to sell for NPC's, For trading valuables items to other players, and to decorate base!

Warehouse

Warehouse is an added feature in bid battles in 18th of November, 2023. It serves as the external storage of a player, it has much larger storage capacity and much cheaper upgrades, though you need to go through your way into warehouse just to make interaction with your storage.
